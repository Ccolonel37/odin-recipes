Ground Turkey Taco

Description

Turkey Tacos – includes a filling of lean ground turkey that’s seasoned with fresh salsa and plenty of spices, then it’s layered into warmed tortillas and finished with all the best classic taco toppings!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Lean ground turkey
  • 3 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • Cilantro
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Paprika
  • Fresh salsa
  • Tortillas
  • Ancho chili powder

Directions

  1. Step 1
  2. Preheat skillet: Heat oil in a 12-inch non-stick skillet over moderately high heat.
  3. Step 2
  4. Brown turkey: Add turkey season with salt and pepper and let lightly brown on bottom, about 3 minutes.
  5. Step 3
  6. Break up turkey and cook through with garlic: Reduce heat slightly, turn turkey, add garlic. Break up turkey and let just barely cook through.
  7. Step 4
  8. Add spices, saute: Chili powder, ancho chili powder, cumin, optional paprika and saute 30 seconds longer.
  9. Step 5
  10. Simmer with salsa: Pour in salsa and toss. Reduce heat to low. Cover and let simmer for a few minutes
  11. Step 6
  12. Serve filling over tortillas with toppings: Serve turkey mixture over individual tortillas topped with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and optional hot sauce.
